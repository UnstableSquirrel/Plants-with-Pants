Plants with Pants is a charity collection of 50 one of one NFTs featuring... plants wearing pants! Each little plant will be up for auction starting at 0.02 ETH (roughly $100) on the Polygon network - the Green blockchain.

90% of all proceeds will be going to One Tree Planted, along with 75% of all royalties from secondary market sales!

Join us in taking care of our beautiful planet, one goofy plant wearing pants at a time.
